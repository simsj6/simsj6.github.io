Stadium Piece

AKA Stairs to Nowhere

Mindfulness Exercise

Life is a series of ups and downs, like this sculpture. This is an opportunity to reflect on your own highs and lows as you walk up and down the stairs.

What are some of your favorite moments of your life?
Moments with friends, family, people on the internet. Moments that you look back on fondly, no matter how much time has passed since. Please share if you're comfortable.

What are some of the struggles that you have faced?
Things you thought were impossible to get through until you were on the other side. Things that may not seem bad to others, but were awful to you. Things that put yourself to the test, either physically, mentally, or emotionally. Please share if you're comfortable.

What inspires you to keep going when times are tough?
Whether it be other people or forms of media. Games that distract or music that releases. Key motivations or simply spite. Please share if you're comfortable.

Where do you want to go in the future?
Whether it be a specific physical place or a personality change, how do you want to live in the future? What will you take with you from your past, and what will you leave behind? Please share if you're comfortable.
